▶ CONTRAST / SAT / BRIGHT PORTRAITS
Best for photos & portraits. Boosting contrast separates skin tones from shadows. Saturation pushes colors toward distinct woods. Use when your image looks washed out.
▶ DITHERING GRADIENTS
Best for smooth gradients & landscapes. Mixes neighboring woods in a checkerboard pattern to fake in-between colors. Makes edges noisy — not ideal for sharp pixel art.
▶ SKIP COLOR TOLERANCE BACKGROUNDS
Best for removing solid backgrounds (e.g. white/green screen). Raises or lowers how strictly the skip color is matched — higher = removes more background, but may eat highlights.
